About a week ago

It’s crazy how much can change in just a week. Seven days may seem short, but when you look back, it often feels like a lifetime. One moment, everything is normal, and then suddenly, everything shifts. A week ago, you might have been in a completely different mindset—excited, stressed, indifferent, or hopeful. Maybe something unexpected happened that turned your world upside down. Perhaps it was a conversation, a decision, or just a feeling that crept in and refused to leave. Whatever it was, it shaped where you are today. It’s funny how time works. Some weeks drag on endlessly, while others blur past in an instant. But no matter how it feels, each week leaves its mark. So take a second and think—where were you about a week ago? How have things changed? What did you learn? And most importantly, where do you want to be in another week? Time moves fast, but reflection keeps us grounded. Let’s make the next seven days count.
